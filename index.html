About us

What we do with
your help!

At Walkin Progress, we empower young minds with the knowledge and skills to shape their futures. From financial literacy and career guidance to mentorship, our programs are designed to inspire educate, and equip communities with the tools to succeed. Together, we're building brighter futures and fostering a generation of financially savvy, career-ready leaders.

Services

How we helping
communities

Mentoring

We match students with mentors in their chosen field and offer monthly sessions on university prep, CV writing, and life skills.

Career Guideline

Each month we explore different industries with guest speakers who share real-life insights and career advice.

Financial Literacy

We teach key financial skills like budgeting, saving, and decision-making to help individuals take control of their money.

Networking

Learn how to build a strong LinkedIn profile and connect with professionals for job shadowing, internships, or employment.
