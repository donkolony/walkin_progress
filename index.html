About us

What we do with
your help!

At Walkin Progress, we empower young minds with the knowledge and skills to shape their futures. From financial literacy and career guidance to mentorship, our programs are designed to inspire educate, and equip communities with the tools to succeed. Together, we're building brighter futures and fostering a generation of financially savvy, career-ready leaders.

Services

How we helping
communities

Financial Literacy

Covers key financial topics monthly, helping individuals build money management skills and make informed decisions

Subject choice and Career Guidelines

Explores different industries on a monthly basis, featuring guest speakers who share their personal journeys and insights into achieving success

Mentoring

Students are paired with dedicated mentors in their desired study or career field. They also participate in monthly sessions covering a wide range of topics, including university applications, CV drafting, and essential life skills.

Networking

Individuals will be taught how to network with industry professionals through LinkedIn and will receive support in securing job shadowing, internships or employment opportunities.
